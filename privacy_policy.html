3. SMS Permission

The App requests permission to read SMS messages only to detect UPI payment transactions (from Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and banks). This feature:

  • Reads SMS locally on your device
  • Never transmits SMS content to any server or third party
  • Only processes messages that look like UPI debit notifications
  • Is entirely optional — you can use the App without granting SMS permission

4. Data Storage

All data (expenses, budgets, categories) is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. We do not operate any servers or cloud storage for user data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties under any circumstances.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the App, which removes the local database from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
