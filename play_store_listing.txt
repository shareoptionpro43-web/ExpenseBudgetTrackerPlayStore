FULL DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars):
BudgetBuddy is a complete home expense and budget management app designed for Indian households. Track every rupee, stay within budget, and automatically import your UPI payments.

KEY FEATURES:

📊 Smart Expense Tracking
• Add expenses manually in seconds
• 16 default categories: Food, Groceries, Transport, Utilities, Healthcare, Shopping, Entertainment and more
• Support for Cash, Card, UPI, Bank Transfer, Wallet payments
• Add notes and mark expenses as recurring

💰 Monthly Budget Management
• Set spending limits per category
• Visual progress bars show budget usage
• Get alerts when approaching limits
• Track overall monthly budget health

📱 Auto-Import UPI Expenses from SMS
• Automatically detects UPI payment SMS from Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM
• Supports all major Indian banks: HDFC, SBI, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB, Canara and more
• Smart category suggestion based on merchant name
• Filter imports by UPI provider and month
• Full transaction details: merchant name, UPI ID, bank account, reference number

📈 Reports & Analytics
• Pie chart showing spending by category
• Bar chart showing daily spending trend
• Category-wise breakdown with percentages
• Export expenses to CSV for Excel/Google Sheets

🔒 Privacy First
• All data stored locally on your device
• SMS is read only on your device — never sent to any server
• No account required, no cloud, no ads

WHAT'S NEW IN VERSION 1.0:
• Initial release with full expense tracking
• UPI SMS auto-import from all major providers
• Monthly budget management
• Analytics and charts

YES

What data is collected?
- Financial info: expense amounts and categories (stored locally, not shared)
- SMS messages: read to detect UPI payments (processed on device, not stored or shared)

Is data encrypted in transit? N/A (no data leaves the device)

Can users request data deletion? Yes (uninstall app removes all data)

=== SMS PERMISSIONS DECLARATION ===

Why does your app need READ_SMS?
BudgetBuddy uses READ_SMS permission to automatically detect UPI payment notifications
from Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and major Indian banks. This helps users
import their spending without manual entry.

Is SMS data processed on-device or sent to servers?
SMS content is processed entirely on the user's device. No SMS data is transmitted
to any server or third party.

Is this feature core to the app or optional?
The SMS import feature is optional. Users can use all other features without
granting SMS permission.
